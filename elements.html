Services

I help teams turn complex data into reliable, decision-ready insight — from modern data platforms to executive dashboards and governance frameworks.

What I Offer

Each engagement is tailored to your goals and maturity level — whether you need a quick dashboard uplift or a full cloud data platform build.

1) Data Platform & Lakehouse Architecture

Design and delivery of scalable Azure/Snowflake platforms with clean layering, strong governance, and reliable pipelines.

  • Azure Lakehouse (Bronze/Silver/Gold) and modern warehouse patterns
  • Pipeline design (Databricks, Synapse, ELT/ETL orchestration)
  • Data modelling for analytics (star schema, semantic layer)
  • Performance and cost optimisation

2) Power BI & Executive Reporting

Professional reporting ecosystems built for adoption, performance, and trust — not just charts.

  • Executive dashboards and operational performance packs
  • DAX optimisation and robust KPI frameworks
  • Embedded analytics (Power BI Embedded) for external users
  • Report performance tuning and modelling best practice

3) Data Governance & Quality

Practical governance that improves trust and reduces rework — aligned to real delivery needs.

  • Data definitions, lineage, and cataloguing support (e.g., Purview)
  • Quality rules, checks, and exception management
  • Access control and secure consumption patterns
  • Documentation standards to support scale and continuity

4) Analytics Automation & Enablement

Reduce manual reporting and upskill teams with repeatable templates and training.

  • Automation patterns for recurring reports and refresh
  • Reusable reporting templates
  • Mentoring for analysts/engineers
  • Process improvements for insight delivery

How I Work

A clear delivery approach keeps stakeholders aligned and outcomes measurable.

  1. Discovery: goals, users, data sources, success metrics, constraints
  2. Design: target architecture, data model, governance, reporting approach
  3. Build: pipelines, models, dashboards, documentation
  4. Validate: testing, performance, reconciliation, stakeholder sign-off
  5. Adoption: training, handover, operating model & improvement plan

Tools & Technology

  • Cloud & Data: Azure, ADLS Gen2, Synapse Analytics, Databricks, Snowflake
  • Analytics: Power BI, Power BI Embedded, DAX, Power Query (M)
  • Engineering: SQL, Python, Terraform, CI-friendly patterns
  • Governance: Purview-aligned cataloging & data quality frameworks
