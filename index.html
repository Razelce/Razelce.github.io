Amara Olisa

I help organisations turn data into a trusted, decision-ready asset. My work sits at the intersection of data strategy, platform architecture, and analytics delivery — translating business priorities into scalable cloud solutions, clear governance, and reporting environments that leaders can rely on.

With 7+ years across data architecture, engineering, and BI, I support teams through modernisation programmes and delivery: defining target-state architecture, building reliable pipelines and models, establishing governance foundations, and enabling adoption through stakeholder engagement and practical training.

My consulting style is structured and outcome-led: clarify objectives, design the right architecture, deliver in increments, and measure impact. I’m particularly strong at simplifying complex environments and aligning technical decisions with what the business actually needs — speed, trust, cost efficiency, and performance visibility.
